GRAPH TRAIN

My submission to the University of Warwick 2023 Game Jam. The game, Graph Train, is a puzzle game where the player must guide a train by writing the line equation for its tracks. I worked on it with Leila Davis and Josh Humphriss. The game won second place for best designed game.
